NCAA DOLT (Capt. Haruna) — Desktop Migration Instructions

1. EXPORT ON OLD LAPTOP:
   - Open NCAA DOLT on your old laptop.
   - Go to "Settings & Backup" in the sidebar.
   - Click "Export Database Backup (.json)" to download your license database.

2. TRANSFER TO NEW LAPTOP:
   - Copy this "Capt-Haruna-Desktop-Bundle" folder AND your downloaded ".json" backup file onto a flash drive.
   - Paste the "Capt-Haruna-Desktop-Bundle" folder onto the Desktop of the new laptop.

3. START APP ON NEW LAPTOP:
   - Double-click "START_CAPT_HARUNA.bat" inside this folder.
   - It will open the application in your web browser.

4. RESTORE DATABASE ON NEW LAPTOP:
   - Log in as the "Assistant" role using your PIN (default or custom).
   - Go to "Settings & Backup" in the sidebar.
   - Under "Restore From Backup File", click "Browse / Choose File" and select the ".json" backup file you copied from the old laptop.
   - Click "Restore" and confirm.
   - All your personnel records, photos, and audit logs will be loaded!
