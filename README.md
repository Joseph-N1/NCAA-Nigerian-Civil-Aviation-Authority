# 🛫 NCAA (Nigerian Civil Aviation Authority)

> A collection of software engineering, data, and AI projects inspired by my National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) experience at the Nigerian Civil Aviation Authority (NCAA).

---

## Overview

This repository documents the projects I designed and developed while serving at the Nigerian Civil Aviation Authority (NCAA).

Rather than treating my NYSC year as an opportunity to simply observe aviation operations, I challenged myself to identify real workflows, understand operational challenges, and build software that explored how technology could support regulatory and engineering activities.

The projects in this repository combine aviation knowledge with software engineering, data visualization, web development, automation, and artificial intelligence.

Although these projects were inspired by real operational environments, they were developed independently as learning and portfolio projects.

---

# Why I Built This Repository

Aviation is one of the most safety-critical industries in the world.

Every inspection...
Every maintenance record...
Every licence...
Every technical decision...

depends on accurate information.

Throughout my NYSC, I became increasingly interested in how software can improve regulatory efficiency, reduce manual work, and support engineers and inspectors.

This repository represents that journey.
